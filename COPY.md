## FAQ Page Copy (Example Questions)

### General
**Q**: What types of businesses do you work with?

**A**: We specialize in small businesses (5–50 employees) and home offices. This includes professional services (law, accounting, consulting), retail, healthcare, hospitality, and creative studios.

---

**Q**: Do you offer emergency support?

**A**: Yes. Managed service clients receive priority support with a 4-hour response time during business hours. Emergency after-hours support is available on a case-by-case basis.

---

### Services
**Q**: Can you upgrade my existing network or do I need to start from scratch?

**A**: In most cases, we can upgrade your existing infrastructure. We'll assess what you have, identify bottlenecks, and integrate new equipment seamlessly.

---

**Q**: Do you support Macs and PCs?

**A**: Yes, we support both macOS and Windows environments, as well as mobile devices (iOS and Android).

---

### Pricing
**Q**: How much does a typical network upgrade cost?

**A**: Most small business network upgrades range from $3,500 to $7,000 depending on the size of your space, number of users, and specific requirements (cameras, VLANs, VPN, etc.). We provide a detailed quote before any work begins.

---

**Q**: Do you offer financing or payment plans?

**A**: We accept payment via check, credit card, or bank transfer. For larger projects, we can discuss milestone-based payment plans.

---

### Technical
**Q**: What brands of equipment do you install?

**A**: We primarily use Ubiquiti UniFi and TP-Link Omada for networking, Synology for storage/backups, and a variety of PoE IP cameras depending on your needs. We choose equipment based on reliability, performance, and long-term support.

---

**Q**: Can I access my security cameras remotely?

**A**: Yes. All camera systems we install include secure remote access via mobile app or web browser. We ensure access is encrypted and protected with strong authentication.

---

### Support
**Q**: What's included in managed support?

**A**: Managed support includes 24/7 network monitoring, automatic backups, security updates, priority troubleshooting (4-hour response), quarterly site visits, and proactive equipment maintenance.

---

**Q**: How quickly can you respond to issues?

**A**: For managed service clients, we guarantee a 4-hour response time during business hours. Non-managed clients are supported on a best-effort basis, typically within 1–2 business days.
